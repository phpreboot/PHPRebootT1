What we do?

PHP Reboot is a community of PHP developers in Pune, India who meet regularly for knowledge shearing technical meetups. We also collect blogs, articles, tutorials on PHP in form of monthly magazine or categories.

Blog & news

Blogs by PHP Reboot community members.

Recent articles added

At PHP reboot, we collect links to recent articles, blogs, free tutorials for community.

Please note, we are not authors, but collectors. All collected articles in PHP Reboot may be licensed under different license and copyright of respective author and/or website. All articles are linked properly to original contents. Please take permission from original author/website before doing anything else then reading.
